About Us

Based in Northern Malaysia, INSYDE CREATIVE is a next-generation agency engineering fresh ideas and high-impact digital solutions.

Originally initiated as Comixes Production in 2024, our system underwent a massive upgrade in 2025. By fusing our core architecture with the digital division of TempatKu PG, we evolved into the powerhouse known today as INSYDE CREATIVE—built to push boundaries and redefine the digital landscape.
